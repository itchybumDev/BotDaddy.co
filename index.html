How we work

Strategy

We always start with the question: "What problem are you going to solve for your end consumer?". On basis of this we create a conversational commerce strategy and define business objectives.

Design

We design a custom flow of the bot and select the most suitable messaging platform. This can be a custom-built platform, Facebook Messenger, WeChat, WhatsApp, Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa or any other voice or chat platform.

Development

We create and develop the bot. All our chat & voice bots are powered with the BotDaddy.co multi-lingual NLP and AI which supports many languages. Our bots are privacy by design and adhere to the strict EU privacy guidelines.

Optimization

We launch and push the bot live. Consumers engage and converse with the bot. Based on customer feedback and data, we continue to optimize, increase conversation and keep growing your conversational channel.

BotDaddy.co specializes in driving business goals for global brands through AI driven chatbots. We help our clients define their strategy for Facebook Messenger, Skype, Telegram, Kik, Zalo and all other chat platforms and we build the chatbot equipped to realize this strategy. After having defined the right strategy and having built the chatbot, we execute the defined chat platform strategy for our clients with an optimal Return On Investment.
